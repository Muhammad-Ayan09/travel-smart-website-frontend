Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a trip with Travel Smart?

You can book a trip through our website by browsing our catalog, selecting your desired destination, and following the booking process. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team directly for assistance with your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some regions, we offer payment plans. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for a full refund if canceled 30 days before the trip date. Cancellations made 15-29 days before receive a 50% refund, and less than 15 days are non-refundable. Some premium packages may have different terms, which will be clearly stated during booking.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance packages that cover trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. We recommend adding insurance to your booking for peace of mind during your travels.
